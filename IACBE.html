IACBE 国际认证

  华南理工大学广州学院是IACBE国际认证的教育会员，致力于通过国际认证全面提升教学质量，以提供卓越的商科教育。

  IACBE（International Assembly for Collegiate Business Education，国际大学商学院教育认证委员会）成立于1997年，是一所非营利性质的学术机构。2011年1月获美国高等教育认证委员会（CHEA）的肯定与许可。其认证的宗旨是：不以相关投入和资源的硬性规定为标准，而是遵循以人才培养目标、培养过程和结果为标准的一个认证过程。旨在为国际上办学优秀的大学商学院提供专业认证。
 
  通过IACBE认证需要完成三个阶段：1.申请成为教育会员；2.取得候选人资格；3.申请认证。我校已于2013年11月完成了认证过程的第一阶段，率先成为IACBE在中国大陆的第一校所教育会员单位。目前，我校正在进行申请认证的第二阶段，即取得候选人资格。
 
 
IACBE Accreditation
 
  Guangzhou College of South China University of Technology is an Educational Member of the International Assembly for Collegiate Business Education (IACBE), and has affirmed its commitment to excellence in business education.
 
  The International Assembly for Collegiate Business Education (IACBE) was founded in 1997 as a leading mission-driven and outcomes-based professional accreditation agency for business and management education in colleges and universities whose primary purpose is excellence in teaching and learning. The IACBE has hundreds of member institutions and campuses worldwide, and has accredited over 1,100 business and business-related programs in the United States, Europe, Asia, the Middle East, Central America, and South America.
 
  The three categories of IACBE membership are: Educational Member, Candidate for Accreditation and Accredited Member. In November, 2013, Guangzhou College of South China University of Technology has been granted Educational Member and is the first Educational Member of IACBE in the People’s Republic of China.Currently the application forcandidate for accreditation is in progress.
